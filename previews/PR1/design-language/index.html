Design Language

Policy

  • Ensure color accessibility (e.g. contrast)
  • Harmonize the light and dark themes
  • Use derived colors with systematic schemes (i.e. reduce magic numbers)
  • Imitate major color schemes
  • Inherit Julia's color identity
